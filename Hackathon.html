FAQ



Helloo Hacks is a three-day long virtual coding experience for coders of all ages. Participants will be spending most of their time preparing for and completing their coding projects individually. Their projects will be focused on bringing social impact in a way that aligns with our mission. We also offer educational workshops on various topics such as computer programming and methods on completing their projects.



People of all ages are able to attend Helloo Hacks. As long as you have an interest in coding, feel free to join!



Our Helloo World team is passionate about bringing people together globally and inspiring fresh minds to contribute to society in ways big and small. We are made up of a diverse group of students from different locations around the world. As an organization, we believe that the passion for a better world is able to unite all persons on this planet. We hope that our impact can provide a way for participants to begin changing their world using technology, no matter where they are.



You should attend Helloo Hacks for numerous reasons. In our workshops, you will learn how to develop components of a product using different tools: website,mobile application, or APIs. You will also create individual projects that align with our selected themes and purpose. You will have the opportunity to bond with coders worldwide by participating in fun games and chat rooms. Lastly, you can listen to panelists about different ways to advocate for social change!
